At Jodi4Ever, we are committed to maintaining a safe and secure environment for all our users. As a matrimony app designed exclusively for adults seeking life partners, we take child protection and online safety extremely seriously.

verified_user Strictly for Adults (18+)

Jodi4Ever is intended solely for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not allow children or minors to use our platform under any circumstances. Our registration process includes:

  • Mandatory age verification during sign-up
  • Periodic profile reviews and audits

block Prohibited Content

We strictly prohibit the following on our platform:

  • Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Child exploitation, harassment, or grooming behavior
  • Attempts to bypass our age verification systems
  • Creation of accounts for or by underage individuals

Any user found violating these policies will be banned immediately and reported to the relevant legal authorities without exception.

report In-App Reporting Tools

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app. Our moderation team reviews all reports promptly and takes necessary actions, including content removal, account suspension, or reporting to law enforcement when required.

security Proactive Protection Measures

We implement the following proactive safeguards:

  • Regular system audits and safety protocol updates
  • User education through safety tips and guidelines

gavel Compliance with Laws

We comply with all applicable child safety and protection laws, including:

  • International standards on CSAM prevention
  • Regional and national reporting requirements
  • Data protection regulations relevant to minors

We maintain open communication channels with law enforcement agencies and promptly respond to legal requests related to child safety concerns.
